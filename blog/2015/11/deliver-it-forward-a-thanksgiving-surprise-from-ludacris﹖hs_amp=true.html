Deliver It Forward: A Thanksgiving Surprise From Ludacris | Roadie

Written by Will Walker | Nov 26, 2015 5:00:00 AM

Nothing feels better than giving back to your community. Ludacris and the Ludacris Foundation gave three deserving families a brighter Thanksgiving, and we partnered with them to bring the surprise to their doors.

 

 

 

A sense of community and neighbors helping neighbors is what Roadie is all about. Each of the Mia, Mapp, and Morris families have faced challenges this year with strength and love, and they deserve a Thanksgiving they’ve always dreamed of having. Not to mention a surprise from Ludacris too!
