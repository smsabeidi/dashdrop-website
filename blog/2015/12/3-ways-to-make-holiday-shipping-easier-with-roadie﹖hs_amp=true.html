Twinkly lights hang from your windows, a bright Christmas tree sits by your fireplace, and holiday parties have been planned in full. But, you’re still waiting for the last piece to put it all together: gifts under the tree.

That’s where we come in. Send your gifts through the Roadie app or on Roadie.com, and get your holiday delivered right to your door. It’s the easiest way to make sure Santa comes down that chimney and that your gifts make it home in time for Christmas.

Get Sending

Want to know how? Here are three ways Roadie helps you avoid a holiday shipping disaster.

  1. Buy Online, Roadie Picks Up In-Store

With click and collect, there’s no need to wait for days for the post office to ship your gifts. Simply, buy online with major retailers, like Target, Apple, and Walmart, then use Roadie to get it delivered directly from the store. Just make sure to select in-store pick up and tell the store you’re having Roadie deliver it!

 

  1. Get the Fragile Stuff Hand-Delivered

 Why risk a broken bowl when you can have a Roadie hand-deliver it door to door for you? Track your stuff in real-time from your phone and breathe easy knowing everything you send is covered up to $10,000. Kind of a no-brainer, right?

 

  1. Send the Too-Big, Too-Heavy Stuff Easier and Cheaper

Maybe you found the perfect TV for your sister’s living room or a set of golf clubs for your dad, but you’ve got no way to get it there on time and for less with a usual shipping company. That’s where we come in.
