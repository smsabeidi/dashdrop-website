From Desktop to Doorstep: Introducing Roadie Web | Roadie

Written by Justin Ratcliffe | Dec 2, 2015 5:00:00 AM

You’ve asked for another way to send your stuff, and now it’s here. We’re thrilled to bring you Roadie Web, a faster and easier way to get what you need, when you need it — all at roadie.com.

Create a Gig at Roadie.com. Track it in real-time on your phone. When you create a delivery online, Roadie Web automatically syncs with your iOS or Android app. Send your stuff from Roadie.com, then track it in real-time on your phone!

All the features you need to experience easier delivery on the web. When you send from Roadie Web, you’ll be able to get an instant estimate, create a Gig, select a Driver, and submit Driver ratings.

Get it there with Roadie. Send a gift to a friend, couches to your new place, or cupcakes to customers — Roadie.com is the fastest, easiest, friendliest way to get your stuff where it’s going.
