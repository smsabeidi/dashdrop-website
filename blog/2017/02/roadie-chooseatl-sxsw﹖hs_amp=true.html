Roadie Joins ChooseATL for SXSW Panel on Innovation

Written by Will Walker | Feb 27, 2017 5:00:00 AM

For the second year, ChooseATL is heading to SXSW and bringing along some of Atlanta’s top brands for the ride. We’re thrilled to join the corporate leaders, musicians, and innovators who shape Atlanta’s rich culture and community in a weekend-long celebration of our hometown.

As part of ChooseATL’s Entrepreneur Invitational, Roadie founder and CEO Marc Gorlin will be speaking at the Big Brands Driving Innovation Panel alongside industry-leaders from some of Atlanta’s top companies. The panel will focus on innovative thinking within corporations and the role startups play in fueling disruption.
