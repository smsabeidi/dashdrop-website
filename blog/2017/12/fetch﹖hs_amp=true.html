Roadie and Fetch Launch Truck-Rental Program in Atlanta

Written by Will Walker | Dec 5, 2017 5:00:00 AM

From wreaths to riding lawn mowers, Roadie Drivers help our community practically send anything across town or across the country. But not all Drivers have a large enough vehicle to deliver oversized items or multiple Gigs at once — and we wanted to help change that.

That’s why we are thrilled to launch a truck-rental program in Atlanta with our friends at Fetch, an on-demand truck and cargo van rental agency.

Roadie Drivers will enjoy a discounted rate on any Fetch truck or van, while Fetch Drivers have access to earning extra cash while on the road. For Roadie Drivers with smaller vehicles, the new partnership gives them the chance to make more money delivering oversized items or multiple Gigs at once.

“With a truck, folks can earn more delivering oversized items, like a riding lawnmower from a local hardware store or a couch to a new home in Old Fourth Ward,” said Marc Gorlin, CEO and Founder of Roadie. “By having a hometown partner in Fetch, Atlanta drivers with smaller vehicles can now rent a truck at an affordable rate and make more on drives they’re already taking.”

Not only does the self-service truck rental agency offer vehicles at the tap of a button, but it also includes free base miles with every rental. With the app, drivers can find a truck parked nearest to them and rent by the hour or the day.

“Our mission at Fetch is to make transportation effortless. For Roadie Drivers, Fetch trucks are available whenever they need them at the push of a button. We’re delighted to partner with Roadie to provide drivers with instant access to trucks for transporting large items,” Adam Steinberg, CEO of Fetch, said.

Roadie Drivers can access the discount under the Promotions section in the app or by visiting fetchtruck.com/roadiefetch.
