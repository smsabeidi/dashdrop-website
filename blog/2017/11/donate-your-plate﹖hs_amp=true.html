#DonateYourPlate | Feed A Family of Four This Holiday Season

Written by Will Walker | Nov 15, 2017 5:00:00 AM

Hunger is often hidden, but that doesn’t mean it isn’t there.

Stagnant wages, rising costs of living, underemployment — all are major reasons why nearly 41 million Americans struggle to put food on the table. Every meal matters, and this Thanksgiving, families in every corner of the country will need help filling their plates.

We can do something to help. This holiday season, we’re asking our community to #DonateYourPlate — send us a license plate for our to-be-filled license plate wall, and we’ll feed a family of four through your local food bank.

Our office should represent our community, which is why we want to showcase license plates from all 50 states. But more than that, we can work hand-in-hand to feed families this Thanksgiving, a time when community matters most.
