The Roadie Holiday Shipping Survival Guide

Your customers are frazzled with holiday shopping lists, endless errands and a busy social calendar. So how can you make their lives easier amid the hustle and bustle of the holiday season?

It’s simpler than you think. Give them (and yourself!) the gift of convenience and time with Roadie’s dynamic door-to-door holiday delivery service. No matter how many holiday seasons you’ve conquered before, Roadie’s holiday shipping survival guide can make it even simpler this year.

Looking to close more sales this year? Customers are more likely to make those big-ticket purchases if they have an easy, affordable way to get gifts home or to the front door of their friends and family — and Roadie is your perfect holiday logistics partner for Christmas, Hanukkah, Kwanzaa and other special days.

We’re cool if the cheerful guy in the red suit gets the credit, but when you need a little extra holiday help, use Roadie to deliver things like:

  • Big, bulky items such as kayaks and bicycles that won’t fit in your vehicle
  • Last-minute gifts and same-day items that need to get there ASAP
  • Food and refreshments for holiday gatherings, company parties, etc.
  • Christmas trees, wreaths and garlands
  • Items from a store across town that has what you need in stock
  • Donations to charities (Goodwill, Toys for Tots, etc.)

Keep Surprise Gifts a Secret

Trying to keep a Christmas delivery a surprise? We got you covered.

Your Roadie Driver can help keep it on the hush by slipping that special item in your customer’s garage or basement, or coordinating with customers as they approach so young kids can be moved into another room while Roadie secretly delivers the goods.

And even if it’s not a secret, Roadie makes sending corporate holiday gifts a breeze for you and your top clients.

Secure Holiday Delivery

Don’t settle for traditional delivery services that might damage precious gifts or leave boxes with laptops, iPhones and other valuable items on the front porch all day.

Roadie’s door-to-door service makes Christmas delivery more secure. Watch your item move in real time on our app, and our delivery confirmation feature lets you know exactly when it arrived and who accepted it.

We automatically provide $100 in coverage on every Gig, and you can purchase an additional $10,000 in coverage through our partner, UPS Capital.

Inventory Restocking & Rebalancing

Nervous about running low on inventory during the peak holiday season? You never want your customers to see coveted items “Out of Stock” when they go online or walk in the door.

Roadie can move items between warehouses and stores — during the day or after hours — to make sure you don’t run out of popular gifts and risk losing sales to another retailer.

Our same-day replenishment service is more nimble and affordable than other options such as last-minute couriers. And our point-to-point model means we aren’t making stops at 20 other locations before we deliver your holiday items.

Holiday Treats for Your Sweet

Spread good cheer with holiday treats for your sweet, your valuable corporate client or anyone else you are grateful for this year.

Roadie is great for delivering gingerbread men, buche de noel, stollen, panettone, latkes, lebkuchen, rosca de reyes or Christmas pudding from your favorite bakery.

Our door-to-door service means your delectable delights will be hand-delivered to your lucky recipient in perfect condition.

Holiday logistics couldn’t be easier with Roadie as your Christmas delivery partner, so pour a glass of eggnog and fire up the Yule Log.

Enjoy the merriest of seasons while Roadie does the heavy lifting.
