Roadie Named TAG Top 10 Innovative Technology Company

Written by Will Walker | Mar 9, 2017 5:00:00 AM

We’re thrilled to have been named one of the TAG Top 10 Innovative Technology Companies in Georgia! The Technology Association of Georgia (TAG) honors the state’s “best of the best” for innovation and contributions to Georgia’s technology community. Roadie was recognized for reimagining a whole new way to ship items locally and nationwide.
