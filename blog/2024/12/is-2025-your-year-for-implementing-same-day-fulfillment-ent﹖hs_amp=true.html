Is 2025 your year for implementing same-day fulfillment? | Roadie

Written by Will Walker | Dec 16, 2024 5:00:00 AM

A new year always offers the opportunity for a fresh start. If you’re a retailer, you’re just past peak season and likely handling post-peak returns processes while taking stock of inventory and assessing the strengths and weaknesses of the holiday rush.

In the rapidly evolving retail landscape, delivery options have become a critical factor that shapes consumer behavior. The truth is that consumers want a variety of delivery options at checkout. Crucially, affordability and speed are of the essence, regardless of package dimensions and distribution center distance. So, as you evaluate your processes and final-mile fulfillment plans for 2025, you might wonder whether you should integrate same-day delivery solutions into your arsenal. 

Read on to explore some key industry trends and how same-day delivery can differentiate your business from the competition.

Economic factors & flexibility 

Economic conditions undoubtedly influence consumer priorities when it comes to delivery options. However, the impact is not as straightforward as you might expect. While cost-consciousness may drive some consumers to opt for slower, cheaper delivery methods, the demand for speed and convenience remains strong across various market segments.

In the not-so-distant past, Gen Z listed sustainability as a critical factor in delivery. It’s still very much a priority, but according to recent McKinsey research, “value upstages values,” given ongoing economic uncertainty and inflation. 

As a retailer, that shouldn’t deter you from offering same-day delivery in 2025. Quite the opposite, in fact. Customers want choices, whether shopping on your website or arranging delivery in-store. 

Shoppers may order products online they don’t need right away, but they’ll also purchase goods that they want within hours. Giving customers delivery options — whether it’s free five-day shipping, an overnight flat rate or same-day delivery — helps meet both their short-term needs and longer-term desires. 

Providing diverse choices, including same-day delivery, can push your business forward in 2025.

Why prioritize same-day delivery in 2025

The surge in e-commerce has also dramatically shifted consumer expectations, with speed becoming a critically important purchase decision criteria. Multiple delivery options allow retailers to cater to various customer needs and preferences. These include: 

  • Meeting diverse customer demands: While some purchases can wait, others are time-sensitive. Providing options ensures you meet the needs of every customer with every timeline. 
  • Competitive advantage: Amazon may have been one of the first to set high standards for delivery speed, but the gap is narrowing … and fast. If you want to keep up, you need to consider adding same-day delivery in 2025. 
  • Increased sales and customer retention: Eighty-two percent of consumers say they’re more likely to buy from a retailer that offers same-day delivery. Additionally, almost a third of retailers reported a 10% revenue increase when they added same-day options; they also reported a 66% improvement in retention rates.
