The Home Depot names Roadie Store Carrier of the Year for Final Mile - 4th year in a row

Written by Heather Hughes | Feb 2, 2024 5:00:00 AM

For the fourth consecutive year, The Home Depot’s Executive Carrier Council has recognized Roadie as the 2023 Store Carrier of the Year for Final Mile. 

This award demonstrates Roadie’s ongoing commitment to providing seamless delivery capabilities, customer satisfaction for The Home Depot’s customers, and the strong partnership between the two companies since launching The Home Depot’s same-day delivery capabilities in 2018.

“The Home Depot trusted us to make the last-mile experience great for their customers seven years ago. We’ve worked hard to deliver on that promise,” said Marc Gorlin, Roadie’s founder and CEO. “Taking home this award for the fourth year in a row is a recognition that we’re doing what we set out to do – to make their customers’ experience stellar, all the way to their front doors.”

“I love that seven years in, we’re still launching new solutions together, always searching for better ways to meet the needs of The Home Depot’s customers. We’re just getting started,” Gorlin said.

Drivers on the Roadie platform deliver everything from screwdrivers to front doors from 1,900+ The Home Depot stores nationwide.
