Roadie earns a spot on Top Workplace list for the fifth consecutive year

Written by Heather Hughes | Mar 20, 2024 4:00:00 AM

ATLANTA, March 2024 – Roadie, Inc., a UPS company, has been named a Top Workplace for 2024, marking an impressive five-year streak. And, for the second time, a Top Workplace USA. 

Roadie, founded in 2014, is an innovative logistics and crowdsourced delivery platform. Collaborating with businesses of every type and size, Roadie enables local deliveries across the U.S. With a network of over 200,000 drivers, Roadie covers more than 29,000 zip codes – the largest local-same-day footprint in the nation.

The Top Workplace list is developed based on anonymous employee feedback gathered through a survey conducted by Energage, LLC – an employee engagement technology company. The survey comprehensively measures 15 vital culture drivers that contribute to an organization’s success and employee satisfaction.

“Our recognition on these lists reflects the vibrant culture we’ve cultivated, the impactful work we’re dedicated to, and the amazing team we’ve created,” said Kayla Duperreault, Roadie’s Head of People Operations. “Five years in a row is a major honor – it never gets old.”

“A Top Workplaces award brings a company’s culture strengths to the national stage, helping them differentiate in a fiercely competitive landscape,” said Eric Rubino, Energage CEO. “It’s a morale-boosting honor for the workforce and an effective magnet for attracting top-tier talent.”

To view the full list, please visit https://topworkplaces.com. For more information on Roadie, please visit Roadie.com.
