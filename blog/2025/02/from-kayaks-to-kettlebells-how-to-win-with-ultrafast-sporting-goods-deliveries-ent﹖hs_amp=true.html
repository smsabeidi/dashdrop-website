From Kayaks to Kettlebells: How to win with ultrafast sporting goods deliveries

Written by Will Walker | Feb 6, 2025 5:00:00 AM

What do kettlebell racks, kayaks and kickboxing bags have in common? They’re all items sold at sporting goods stores, and they’re all big, bulky and hard to manage when it comes to transport. And whether a customer is planning the kayaking trip of a lifetime or working on a new strength-training program, timing can be everything. For outdoor enthusiasts and gymgoers alike, access to the right gear and equipment at the right time can make or break their experience — and for companies, it can mean the difference between customer loyalty and a lost sale.

From fishing poles and tents to skis and pilates reformers, ultrafast, same-day delivery is the differentiator in the sporting goods sphere. But these oddly shaped, often unwieldy items also come with their own set of challenges. That’s where Roadie can help you bridge the gap. 

Formulating a game plan with sporting goods fulfillment 

The sporting goods market continues to grow at a modest clip. Fall 2024 projections from Persistence Market Research forecast a global market growth rate in the sporting goods industry of 8.9% from 2024 to 2031. But how can retailers keep up with delivery solutions for big and bulky packages? After all, a pickleball paddle can fit in a carrier-approved box, but a set of golf clubs or a weight bench aren’t nearly as simple to package for delivery. Unlike standard packages, oversized sporting goods come with the following considerations: 

  • Special handling of items like a kayak or baseball pitching machine. 
  • Logistical coordination, which can prove tricky with odd shapes and sizes that won’t fit in certain delivery vehicles. 
  • Additional shipping fees tacked on by traditional carriers, potentially deterring customers from ordering in the first place.

Conventional delivery methods often fall short of customer expectations — taking days or even weeks for really large items when customers have come to expect one- to two-day delivery. This gap presents an opportunity for retailers to adopt innovative solutions like Roadie.

Teeing up to reach more customers 

Whether you’re a midsize sporting goods retailer or a large conglomerate, the benefits can outweigh the costs when offering customers same-day, oversized delivery options. With ultrafast delivery, retailers can deliver a fishing pole and tackle box to a customer’s grandson for his birthday. Or, they could deliver a set of paddle boards the same day to a customer going on a last-minute vacation. These time-sensitive sales can help win you customer brand loyalty for life. 

Every customer order is an opportunity to create happiness and satisfaction, and the bottom line is that offering same-day, oversized delivery can help boost your sales, giving customers personalized delivery options and convenient final-mile fulfillment.
