Roadie is Walmart’s top partner for driver quality and customer NPS | Roadie

Written by Randi Prince | Dec 15, 2020 5:00:00 AM

We’re excited to announce Walmart named Roadie their top delivery partner for “Highest Driver Customer Satisfaction” and “Highest Net Promoter Score” in 2020. 

Just two years after launching same-day grocery delivery for Walmart, we’ve expanded into general merchandise and hundreds more store locations — not just in major cities, but in the smaller cities and towns other delivery providers can’t reach.

We invest a lot in driver success because delivery is one of the most important touchpoints in the entire customer experience. It’s nice to hear the world’s largest retailer thinks we’re doing something right.
