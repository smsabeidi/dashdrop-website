Get Started

It’s not too late to launch same-day delivery. Roadie can help.

by Marc Gorlin
SHARE

Just like you, I’m closely monitoring the rapidly developing news around COVID-19. As the father of two teens and a 5-year old delivery start-up, I’m thinking about safety almost constantly.

Here at Roadie, our team is working overtime to get drivers and customers the information and tools they need to keep themselves, and each other, safe. In addition to ramping up communications, we’ve made changes to our internal systems and operating procedures and are asking our business partners to do the same.

But we’re not just playing defense. These are unprecedented times, and we feel incredibly lucky to have the opportunity to make a positive impact. Now more than ever, we’re finding a way to say YES. Roadie is here and scaling delivery capacity daily to meet demand for groceries, pharmacy, medical supplies, home goods and more. As an essential service provider, we’re meeting that challenge, with near 99% on-time-delivery nationwide.

I’m continuing to hear from so many that need help getting essential goods out to customers and local communities. If you or someone you know could benefit from our platform now, please contact us today.
