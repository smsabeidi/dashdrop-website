Our web app for senders makes delivery with Roadie better. | Roadie

Written by Justin Ratcliffe | Mar 9, 2020 4:00:00 AM

Since launching our new Roadie Delivery app, we’ve gotten great feedback from our customers. Personal and business customers agree the web experience makes delivery with Roadie better — whether you’re on a computer or your mobile device.Last fall, we launched our new Roadie Delivery app: a web-based experience, optimized to work great from any device. 

Since then, we’ve gotten a lot of good feedback. Our customers, both for personal and business use, agree the new and improved experience makes sending stuff with Roadie better — whether you’re on a desktop computer or your mobile device. 

Beginning next month, our iOS and Android apps will no longer be available to senders. But don’t worry — you can still post and track deliveries on your mobile device. You’ll see all the same features you’ve come to rely on, plus new features to ensure every delivery goes smoothly, from pickup to drop off. 

You can learn more in our new Sender Guide.

Still prefer tapping a button on your device’s screen? No problem. Log in to my.roadie.com/send on your smartphone and you’ll see all your account information and Gig history, just as you always do. You can follow the on-screen prompt to add a Roadie Web button to your home screen.

We appreciate your business here at Roadie, and we’re always working to make your user experience better. We look forward to sharing more updates with you down the road!
