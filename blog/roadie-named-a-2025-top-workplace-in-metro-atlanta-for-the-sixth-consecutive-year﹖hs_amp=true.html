Roadie Named a 2025 Top Workplace in Metro Atlanta for the Sixth Consecutive Year

Written by Heather Hughes | Aug 19, 2025 11:00:00 AM

ATLANTA, AUGUST 19, 2025 – For the sixth straight year, Roadie, Inc., a UPS company, has been recognized as a Top Workplace in Metro Atlanta by the Atlanta Journal-Constitution.

Roadie is proud to be recognized for its workplace culture — an honor that comes directly from the voices of its team, not from financial performance or industry accolades.

The recognition is based on a confidential survey conducted by workplace research firm Energage, which measures how respected, supported, and empowered employees feel, all key drivers of engagement and organizational success.

This achievement reflects the experiences and feedback of Roadie’s team members and underscores the company’s commitment to building a workplace where people are valued, heard, and truly at the heart of everything the company does.

“Our focus has always been creating a workplace where people feel supported, trusted, and able to grow,” said Kayla Duperreault, VP, People Operations at Roadie. “Earning this recognition year after year shows we haven’t just set a high standard — we’ve stayed true to it. It’s extremely meaningful because it reflects how our team continues to experience that culture every day, even through our growth.”

“Earning a Top Workplaces award is a badge of honor for companies, especially because it comes authentically from their employees,” said Eric Rubino, CEO of Energage. “That’s something to be proud of. In today’s market, leaders must ensure they’re allowing employees to have a voice and be heard. That’s paramount. Top Workplaces do this, and it pays dividends.”

To learn more about the 2025 Top Workplaces list, visit https://topworkplaces.com. To learn more about Roadie, visit www.roadie.com.
