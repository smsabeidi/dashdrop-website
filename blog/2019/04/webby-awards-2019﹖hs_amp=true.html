Roadie is nominated for The Webby Awards and you can help us win

Written by Will Walker | Apr 3, 2019 4:00:00 AM

Big news for our team today! Roadie is nominated for The Webby Awards, also known as the Oscars of the Internet, in the best general website: web services & applications category.

The Webby Awards honor the Best of the Internet, and we made the shortlist from over 13,000 submissions across 70 countries. And we’re also in unbelievably amazing company: Lady Gaga, Childish Gambino, Will Smith, Funny or Die, Fortnite, Rotten Tomatoes, Pinterest, Red Bull and more.

But we need your help to win! Head over to the Webby Awards website and cast your vote:

  1. Go to our category page
  2. Click or tap Vote on Roadie
  3. Enter your information
  4. Confirm your vote via email (it’s the only way it’ll count!)

And if your friends and family want to vote too, we’re always supportive of sharing the love!

In partnership with Narwhal Digital, we redesigned the Roadie website to better attract and convert the large, retail brands we now work with, while still serving the everyday drivers and senders that have been part of our community for the last four years.  From educational content and driver spotlights to success stories and service-offering overviews, our site has matched the evolution of our company without sacrificing the friendliness of who we are.
