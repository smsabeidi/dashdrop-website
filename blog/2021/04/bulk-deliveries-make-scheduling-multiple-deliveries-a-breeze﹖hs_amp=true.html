Getting started with Bulk Upload

To get started, log in to your sender dashboard. From your dashboard, click “Bulk Upload” in the left-hand sidebar.

 

 

 

 

Our CSV template makes it easy

Organize all of your deliveries using our downloadable CSV template. Once you upload your file, we’ll double check for potential errors.

 

 

Crosscheck details and track from your dashboard

Before you create your deliveries, it’s easy to crosscheck customer names, addresses, order numbers and more. You’ll also see the price for each delivery, as well as the overall cost for every order in your file. As deliveries are created and drivers are assigned, you can see live tracking for each delivery from your dashboard.

 

Bulk Upload makes scheduling multiple deliveries easier. And with our routing feature, similar deliveries heading in the same direction may be grouped together with a single driver, which makes pick-ups a lot easier to manage.
