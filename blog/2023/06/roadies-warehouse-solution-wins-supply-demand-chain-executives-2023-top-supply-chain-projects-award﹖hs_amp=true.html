Roadie’s Warehouse Solution wins Supply & Demand Chain Executive’s 2023 Top Supply Chain Projects Award

Written by Heather Hughes | Jun 15, 2023 4:00:00 AM

Roadie’s Roadie Direct™ warehouse delivery solution has won this year’s Top Supply Chain Projects Award from Supply & Demand Chain Executive. We’re taking local same-day and next-day delivery to the warehouse floor.

Roadie Direct Warehouse Delivery is a first-of-its-kind solution that helps retailers better leverage their existing warehouse footprint to unlock faster-than-ever delivery speeds without taking on additional infrastructure investments. 

The solution includes Roadie’s proprietary batching and route-building algorithm, in-house sortation with the new Roadie SmartSort™ app, and out-of-the-box integration with Roadie’s crowdsourced delivery platform.

Combined, these products create a single packaged solution that enables efficient warehouse-to-doorstep deliveries while eliminating middle-mile costs for retailers.
