It’s official: spring cleaning is on our minds. Spring has sprung, and now is the time to clean out closets, scrub the floors, and donate what you don’t need. Until May 31st, donate items to a local charity, and Roadie will deliver for free!

Whether you’re donating to Goodwill, Dress for Success, or The Salvation Army, give back to your community while you’re cleaning out your closet.

Send Anything With Roadie

Get Sending

It’s simple. Use code SPRING20, post a Gig, and Roadie will deliver door-to-door for free. Just make sure to give your local charity a shout out in the Gig description!

Have a friend that needs to spring clean too? Tell your friends about Roadie. They’ll get $10 toward their first Gig, and you get $10 when they complete it!
