Five Easy Ideas for Random Acts of Kindness | Roadie

Written by Will Walker | Feb 17, 2016 5:00:00 AM

People helping people lives at the heart of what we do, so it only makes sense that our Roadie team can help you do the same. Whether it’s surprising your co-workers or giving back to a good cause, this year on National Random Acts of Kindness Day, pay it forward today and every day.

We’ll even help you by delivering kindness right to someone’s door! Check out five easy ideas for random acts of kindness and make someone’s day.

Send Anything With Roadie

Get Sending

1. Donate clothes to your favorite local charity

Looking to make room in your closet? There’s no better way to clear it out and put a smile on someone’s face than by donating your clothes to a local Goodwill or Salvation Army. You do some good, and we’ll deliver it door-to-door.

2. Buy some extra groceries for the local food bank

Before checking out at the grocery store, grab a few extra boxes to donate to your local food bank. Helping fight hunger doesn’t cost much, and getting it there won’t be hard with Roadie.

3. Thank your coworkers with a sweet treat

Nothing shows your appreciation for your co-workers like getting them a box full of cookies delivered right to the office kitchen. Show your colleagues how much you like working with them and surprise them with an afternoon treat.

4. Help out our furry friends and donate to an animal shelter

From kitten food to puppy training pads, you’d be surprised how much shelters need to give animals a good place to live. Send some treats and toys to an animal shelter near you, and give our furry friends something great to bark about.

5. Send your furniture to a friend

Know a friend looking for a new couch or just trying to make room for your new one? Rather than selling it, do your friend a solid and send your furniture to them for free. And if you don’t want to give it to a friend, just donate it.
