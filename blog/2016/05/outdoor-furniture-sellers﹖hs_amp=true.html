Summer is almost here, but is your outdoor space ready for the heat? We found five of our favorite outdoor furniture sellers across the country to help you create the patio of your dreams. And we’ll even help you get it home for less!

Brian Boggs Chairmakers

Near the forests in Asheville, NC, Brian Boggs Chairmakers specializes in handcrafted wooden tables and chairs. His work has been celebrated internationally, and based on these patio looks, we couldn’t help but fall in love with his furniture.

Radical Relics

Considered one of the best online vintage furniture dealers, Radical Relics has made a name for itself on Instagram with their one-of-a-kind pieces. You can buy their stuff via their Instagram or at Urban Americana!

Firehouse Antiques
Firehouse Antiques calls New Harmony home, but owner Cindy Smotherman travels the country to find one-of-a-kind pieces for her customers. From wicker furniture to midcentury modern dressers, Cindy’s customers know they can always count on Firehouse Antiques for incredible patio furniture finds.
                 
Fancy Willow

Fancy Willow prides itself on handmade outdoor furniture that adds “great casual appeal to any décor.” We love that Fancy Willow believes that conservation is just as important as productivity when creating their furniture.

 

S&S Fire Pits

We have loved S&S Fire Pits ever since they posted their first Gig on Roadie. Handcrafted from the best materials, Andy truly enhances the outdoor living space surrounding your home.

OnPoint Wood Design

There is no doubt these handmade farm tables are 100% American made. OnPoint Wood Design prides itself on custom farm tables, dining sets and patio tables to fit any and every occasion, especially for the outdoors.

Industrial Envy

For Industrial Envy, the vision began with the idea of an American manufacturing revolution. Industrial Envy makes all of its furniture by hand with materials locally sourced in Texas. And to be honest, we couldn’t help but fall in love with these porch swings.

 

Patio Furniture Plus

Patio Furniture Plus is family owned, and they have everything you could possibly need for your patio! From outdoor umbrellas to barbecue pits, it’s easy to get your outdoor furniture fix with them.
