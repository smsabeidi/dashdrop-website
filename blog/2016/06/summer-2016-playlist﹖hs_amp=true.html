Our Ultimate Summer 2016 Playlist | Roadie

Written by Will Walker | Jun 3, 2016 4:00:00 AM

A new month means new tunes. And this month, we put together the Ultimate Summer 2016 Playlist for pool days, road trips, and everything in between.

Each playlist has a different vibe because let’s face it — you won’t always be in the mood for guitar-slinging country music or EDM festival tunes. So whether you’re taking a cross-country road trip or hanging out at home, take this playlist on your summer adventures.
