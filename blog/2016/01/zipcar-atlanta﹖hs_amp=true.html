We Teamed Up with Zipcar Atlanta | Roadie

Written by Will Walker | Jan 19, 2016 5:00:00 AM

The collaborative economy just got a bit more collaborative. Atlanta Roadies, meet the Atlanta Zipsters.

We’re excited to team up with our friends at Zipcar, the world’s leading car sharing network, to give our Atlanta communities the best wheel deals and fastest deliveries in the city.

Zipcar Atlanta will now offer a special discount on new memberships for Atlanta Roadies, while Atlanta Zipsters can now send their first Roadie delivery for free.

Similar to how we’re changing the way people send their stuff, Zipcar has long been doing the same for how people get around cities.

With both apps in tow, you’ve now got an easy way to get your “wheels when you want them” and get your stuff where it needs to go!

It’s just one more way for us to say thank you for being part of the Roadie family — and to make your experience a great one both on and off the road.
