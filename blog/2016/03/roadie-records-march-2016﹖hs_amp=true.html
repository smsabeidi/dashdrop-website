Written by Will Walker | Mar 9, 2016 5:00:00 AM

New month means new tunes. Every month we’ll bring you Roadie Records, a new playlist that’s perfect for the road.

Each playlist has a different vibe because let’s face it — you won’t always be in the mood for indie-pop-acoustic tunes played with only guitars.

But this month, we’re kicking off Roadie Records with just that: an easy, indie pop-inspired playlist. Think Lord Huron to First Aid Kit and even some Coldplay thrown in there.
