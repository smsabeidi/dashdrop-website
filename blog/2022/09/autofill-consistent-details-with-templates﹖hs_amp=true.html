How it Works

Signing up for a custom template is easy. Fill out our quick form and we’ll get in touch for the rest! You’ll tell us the details you’d like to keep consistent, such as your delivery category, deadline, or delivery size. You can also pre-fill special instructions, like where to pick up or where to park, and even lock certain fields that never change, like your pickup address.

Once we’ve created your template, you’ll see all of your pre-filled info when you go to create a delivery.

Have a special order? No worries – unlocked fields can still be edited, so there’s no issue if you need to make a change.

Templates allow you to ensure consistency across all of your deliveries and streamline your sending experience. High-volume senders can improve their experience even further with our time-saving Bulk Upload and cost-cutting Batch Delivery features.
