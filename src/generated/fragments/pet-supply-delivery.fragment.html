Paw-some Perks for Pet Care Businesses

Roadie enables your business with a range of benefits to help you optimize delivery operations and enhance the customer experience.

Any Item

Deliver Pet Products of All Sizes

From cat toys to large bags of food and bulky crates, Roadie allows you to handle the delivery of almost any pet-related item. The network includes a variety of vehicles and drivers with experience handling pet products.

Nationwide Coverage

Reach Pet Owners Everywhere

Expand your delivery radius and reach more customers with the extensive Roadie network. Cover 97% of U.S. households across 30,000+ zip codes, ensuring you can deliver pet products to nearly every corner of the country.

Flexible Delivery

Deliver from Stores or DCs

Optimize same-day operations with delivery from stores, warehouses, DC or FCs. Offer your customers flexible delivery options to meet their needs. Send up to 30 orders with one driver picking up from stores, or send LTL/FTL trailers to RoadieXD™ sortation cross-docks for delivery by end of day.

Better Experiences

Enhance Customer Satisfaction

Get high-quality, reliable deliveries that are handled with care and professionalism. Plus, use real-time tracking and white-labeled notifications to create amazing delivery experiences that foster loyalty.

Greater Efficiency

Optimize the Last Mile

Competitive pricing and efficient operations help you optimize your delivery costs. UPS customers can also bill Roadie deliveries to their UPS account to count toward small package contract commitments and tiers.

Seamless Integration

Connect to Any System

Integrate with your existing systems quickly and easily. Roadie is also available in select UPS shipping systems including the UPS API, ConnectShip, Delivery Solutions – with more coming soon.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can Roadie help my pet care business?

Roadie provides flexible and reliable delivery solutions to help your pet care business meet customer demand, offer convenient delivery options and streamline your operations.

What types of pet care deliveries can Roadie handle?

Roadie enables you to handle a wide variety of pet care deliveries, including pet food (bags and bulk), pet medications, toys, supplies and even larger items like pet crates or carriers.

How does Roadie ensure the safe and timely delivery of pet care items?

Roadie's platform allows for clear communication with drivers, specific delivery instructions and real-time tracking to ensure pet care items are delivered safely and on time.

How does Roadie's pricing for pet care delivery work?

Roadie pricing for pet care delivery varies based on factors such as distance, size and weight of the items and delivery urgency. You'll receive a transparent quote before confirming your delivery. Create a free business account to quote a delivery or contact the Roadie sales team for volume pricing.

What are the benefits of using Roadie for pet care delivery?

Using Roadie for pet care delivery allows you to offer convenient delivery options to your customers, expand your delivery range, handle large or heavy items and improve overall customer satisfaction with industry-leading on-time delivery performance.

How can I get started with Roadie for my pet care deliveries?

You can get started with Roadie by creating a free business account or by contacting our sales team for a customized solution.
