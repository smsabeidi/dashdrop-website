Frequently Asked Questions

What types of e-commerce deliveries can I make with Roadie?

Roadie allows you to deliver just about anything, including clothing, electronics, books, furniture, small appliances, exercise equipment, home goods, artwork, flowers, jewelry, graphics and more.

How does Roadie integrate with my e-commerce platform?

Roadie integrates seamlessly with various e-commerce platforms, including Shopify. You can also integrate Roadie using a public API. 

How does Roadie ensure the safe and timely delivery of my e-commerce orders?

Roadie prioritizes the safe and timely delivery of your e-commerce orders through several measures:

  • Driver Screening: All Roadie drivers undergo a thorough screening process before getting matched with deliveries.
  • Vehicle Requirements: Drivers must have appropriate vehicles for transporting e-commerce orders, ensuring cleanliness and proper storage.
  • Real-time Tracking: Advanced tracking technology provides full visibility into the location and status of each delivery.
  • Secure Handling: E-commerce businesses can include custom handling instructions so drivers handle with care and ensure their security during transit.
  • Delivery Confirmation: Both you and your customer receive confirmation upon successful delivery, including photo proof of delivery and optional signature.

How does pricing for e-commerce delivery work?

Roadie's pricing for e-commerce delivery is based on several factors, including delivery location, distance, size and urgency. Create a free business account and get an instant quote or contact the Roadie sales team for volume pricing details.

What are the benefits of using Roadie for e-commerce delivery?

Using Roadie for e-commerce delivery offers numerous advantages:

  • Increased Customer Satisfaction: Faster and more reliable delivery improves the customer experience and enhances loyalty.
  • Improved Efficiency: The Roadie platform integrates seamlessly with existing e-commerce systems, optimizing the delivery process.
  • Scalability: The Roadie nationwide network can handle fluctuations in delivery volume, allowing reliable delivery even during peak seasons.
  • Nationwide Coverage: Roadie is available in all 50 states, providing a consistent solution across your entire customer base.

How can I get started with Roadie for my e-commerce deliveries?

Getting started with Roadie is simple. Businesses can create a free account at send.roadie.com. Sending more than 25 deliveries a week? Contact the Roadie sales team to get even more features designed to further streamline your last-mile logistics. 

Can Roadie help with age-verification for e-commerce deliveries that require it? (e.g., alcohol, tobacco)

Yes, Roadie can assist with age-verification for e-commerce in states where age-verification deliveries are allowed. Drivers can be certified to verify the recipient's age upon delivery, ensuring compliance with regulations and preventing underage access to restricted products. Contact Roadie sales for more information.
