Frequently Asked Questions

What types of home decor items can businesses deliver with Roadie?

Roadie enables you to deliver a wide range of home decor items including vases, candles, mirror, artwork, shelves, rugs, throw pillows, lamps, pendant lights, chairs, bed frames, patio furniture, planters and more!

How do drivers handle fragile or delicate home decor items?

You can include special handling instructions in delivery notes for fragile items. Drivers can see delivery notes ahead of pickup and know to treat fragile items with caution, ensuring they arrive at their destination in perfect condition.

Can businesses deliver oversized or bulky home decor items, like furniture or rugs, with Roadie?

Yes, Roadie allows you to handle the delivery of oversized or bulky home decor items. The network includes a variety of vehicles, from cars and SUVs to cargo vans and box trucks, to accommodate different sizes and weights. When creating your delivery, please provide accurate dimensions and weight information, and you’ll get matched with a driver with the appropriate vehicle.

How does Roadie pricing for home decor delivery work?

Pricing for same-day delivery is based on several factors, including:

  • Delivery location
  • Delivery distance
  • Item size and weight
  • Delivery urgency

What are the benefits of using Roadie for home decor delivery?

Roadie offers several benefits for home decor delivery:

  • Faster Delivery: Same-day and next-day delivery options ensure timely delivery of items.
  • Improved Efficiency: Streamline your delivery operations and reduce delays with efficient consolidations and real-time tracking.
  • Increased Flexibility: Handle a wide range of delivery needs, from small accessories to furniture, with a diverse network of independent drivers and vehicles.
  • Enhanced Customer Satisfaction: Provide a positive delivery experience for your customers with reliable and convenient service.

How can I get started with Roadie for my home decor deliveries?

Getting started with Roadie is easy:

  • Setup your Account: Businesses can create free accounts at send.roadie.com. Sending more than 25 deliveries a week? Contact the Roadie Sales Team to discuss your delivery needs and explore even more tools built to make same-day delivery more efficient and reliable.
  • Start Delivering: Begin creating and managing your deliveries with the Roadie easy-to-use platform.
