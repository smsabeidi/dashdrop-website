Same-Day Delivery

More Reliable & Efficient Same-Day Delivery

Roadie allows you to make same-day delivery easy and efficient, no matter what you sell or where your customers live. Reach 97% of U.S. households with flexible delivery options and a nationwide network.

Key Features

Roadie offers a comprehensive suite of features designed to make same-day delivery easy and efficient.

Speed

Deliver As Fast As 2 Hours

Offer your customers some of the fastest delivery options available. With Roadie, you can provide 2-hour, 4-hour, or end-of-day delivery, ensuring your customers get what they need when they need it.

Flexibility

Almost Any Item, Anywhere

Deliver almost any item, up to 12 feet in length and 300 lbs (up to 150 lbs for consumers), to 30,000+ zip codes across the U.S. The Roadie network makes it possible to reach nearly every customer with same-day delivery.

Efficiency

Consolidated Deliveries

Optimize your delivery operations with efficient consolidations. The platform groups multiple deliveries for individual drivers, maximizing productivity and streamlining operations.

Scalability

Deliver at Scale

Whether you're handling a few dozen or thousands of deliveries per day, Roadie lets you scale to meet your needs. The flexible platform and nationwide network ensure smooth and reliable same-day delivery, no matter the volume.

Launch Fast

Easy to Integrate

Get started with same-day delivery quickly and easily. Create deliveries online, by bulk upload, via API or through select UPS systems and integrate seamlessly with your existing infrastructure.

Customer Satisfaction

Delight Your Customers

Offer a premium delivery experience that keeps your customers happy. Real-time tracking, flexible delivery options, and reliable service ensure a positive experience that fosters loyalty.

A UPS Company

Integrated with Your UPS Systems

UPS customers can bill Roadie deliveries to their UPS account number, allowing Roadie deliveries to contribute to small package revenue commitments and tiers. Plus, Roadie deliveries can be manifested through select UPS systems.

Increase Sales

Boost Conversions, Sell More

Offering same-day delivery can significantly increase cart conversions and drive sales. Fast and reliable delivery with Roadie gives your customers the instant gratification they crave, leading to higher purchase rates.

Optimize Delivery

Higher On-Time Performance, Fewer Damages

Roadie allows you to optimize your shipping costs with efficient consolidations, high OTD and low damage rates. Reduce friction for your customers and  save on the costs of exceptions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cut-off time for same-day delivery?

The Roadie platform is flexible and businesses can configure their own cut-off times to suit their specific needs and operating hours. Generally, you want to set cut-off times 2 hours before your pickup location closes for the day. For example, if your location closes at 5:00 PM, you can set a cut-off time of 3:00 PM.

What are the different delivery speed options for same-day delivery?

Roadie can be configured for 2-hour, 4-hour or end-of-day deliveries. With multiple options, you can choose the time frame that best balances speed and cost. 

How much does same-day delivery cost with Roadie?

Pricing for same-day delivery is based on several factors, including:

  • Delivery location
  • Delivery distance
  • Item size and weight
  • Delivery urgency
Roadie offers competitive rates and a transparent pricing model, so you'll always know the cost upfront. You can get an instant quote by creating a business account or contacting the Roadie sales team for volume pricing.

Can I track my same-day deliveries with Roadie?

Yes, Roadie enables real-time tracking for all same-day deliveries. You and your customers can monitor the progress of the delivery, receive updates on its status, and get estimated arrival times.

Which Roadie product is right for my same-day deliveries?

Roadie offers two main products for same-day delivery:

  • Roadie Same Day®: Ideal for on-demand delivery within 4-6 hours or for ship-from-store deliveries.
  • RoadieXD™: Designed for high-volume same-day delivery from fulfillment centers leveraging a network of cross-docks for greater efficiency. RoadieXD™ requires a minimum of 50 deliveries per pickup location.
The Roadie sales team can help you assess your needs and recommend the most suitable solution.

What are the benefits of offering same-day delivery to my customers?

Offering same-day delivery with Roadie can provide several benefits for your business:

  • Increased sales: Same-day delivery can be a powerful incentive for customers, leading to higher conversion rates and increased revenue.
  • Improved customer satisfaction: Meeting the growing demand for fast delivery can improve customer satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Competitive advantage: Offering same-day delivery can help you stand out from the competition and attract new customers.
  • Enhanced brand image: Providing a premium delivery experience can enhance your brand image and build trust with your customers.
By offering same-day delivery with Roadie, you can meet customer expectations, improve your business operations, and gain a competitive edge in the market.
