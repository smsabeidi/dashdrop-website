Local Next-Day Delivery

Offer Next-Day Delivery, Without the Rush

Roadie allows you to make next-day delivery easy and efficient. Pick and pack your local next-day orders until the morning of the delivery deadline and reach 97% of U.S. households with flexible delivery solutions and a nationwide network.

Key Features

Roadie includes a comprehensive suite of features designed to make your local next-day delivery offering a breeze.

Extended Cutoffs

More Time to Prepare

Maximize efficiency with extended order cut-offs. Roadie orders are delivered the same-day they’re picked-up. That means you can take, pick and pack local next-day orders until the morning of delivery, giving your fulfillment process more time and reducing stress.

Any Item

Deliver Almost Anything

Businesses can deliver items up to 12 feet in length and 300 lbs (up to 150 lbs for consumers), ensuring you can handle a wide range of products. From small furniture to new tires, the Roadie network allows you to accommodate any local next-day delivery needs.

Local Delivery

Deliver up to 100 Miles

Use Roadie Same Day® to enable local next-day delivery within 100 miles of your store locations. High-volume businesses can inject deliveries into RoadieXD™ cross-docks for delivery within a 100 miles of each cross-dock.

Wide Reach

Nationwide Coverage

Reach customers across the U.S. with the extensive Roadie network. Cover 30,000+ zip codes, ensuring you can offer local next-day delivery to the vast majority of your customer base.

Efficiency

Consolidated Deliveries

Optimize your delivery operations with efficient consolidations. The Roadie platform groups multiple deliveries for individual drivers, maximizing productivity and creating efficiency.

Scalability

Deliver at Scale

Whether you're handling a few dozen or thousands of deliveries per day, Roadie lets you scale to meet demand. The flexible platform and nationwide last-mile network create smooth and reliable local next-day delivery experiences, no matter the volume.

Easy Launch

Quick and Simple Setup

Get started with local next-day delivery quickly and easily. Create deliveries online, by bulk upload, via API or through select UPS systems and integrate seamlessly.

Operational Efficiency

Streamline Your Fulfillment

Local next-day delivery solutions help you streamline your fulfillment process. Extended cut-off times with Roadie Same Day® or RoadieXD™ allow you to pick and pack orders until the day of delivery, maximizing efficiency and reducing operational burdens to offering next-day delivery to your customers.

Customer Satisfaction

Delight Your Customers

Offer a premium delivery experience that keeps your customers happy. With real-time tracking, flexible delivery options and reliable delivery you can ensure a positive experience that fosters loyalty.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cut-off time for next-day delivery?

Roadie's platform is flexible, and businesses can configure their own cut-off times to suit their specific needs and operating hours. Using Roadie Same Day® or RoadieXD™ for local next-day orders, businesses can usually accept orders overnight to be picked and packed the following morning. Orders with Roadie are delivered the same day they are picked up, so local next-day orders can be picked-up the morning of the delivery date. 

How much does local next-day delivery cost with Roadie?

Enjoy peace of mind with flat-rate pricing, based on your delivery’s size, distance, urgency and pick-up location. Log in to the Roadie platform to get an instant quote or contact the Roadie sales team for volume pricing. 

Can I track my local next-day deliveries with Roadie?

Yes, Roadie enables real-time tracking for all deliveries. You and your customers can monitor the progress of the delivery, receive updates on its status and get estimated arrival times on any device.

What are the benefits of offering local next-day delivery to my customers?

Offering local next-day delivery with Roadie can provide several benefits for your business:

  • Increased sales: Local next-day delivery can be a compelling offering for customers, leading to higher conversion rates and increased revenue.
  • Improved customer satisfaction: Meeting customer expectations for fast and reliable delivery can improve satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Competitive advantage: Offering local next-day delivery can help you stand out from competitors and attract new customers.
  • Operational efficiency: Extended cut-off times and streamlined processes can improve your fulfillment efficiency.

How does local next-day delivery with Roadie compare to other options?

Compared to other options, local next-day delivery with Roadie offers several advantages:

  • Cost-effectiveness: For orders less than 100 miles from your pick-up location, Roadie can be more efficient than express delivery, especially for large or bulky items.
  • Flexibility: Roadie allows you more flexibility in terms of item size and cut-off times compared to traditional carriers and couriers.

Which Roadie product is right for my next-day deliveries?

Roadie offers two main products for local next-day delivery:

  • Roadie Same Day®: While primarily designed for same-day delivery, Roadie Same Day® can also be used for next-day deliveries, especially for smaller-scale or on-demand shipments. With Roadie Same Day®, you can pick and pack local next-day orders until the morning of the delivery deadline.
  • RoadieXD™: Ideal for high-volume local next-day deliveries, leveraging a network of cross-docks for greater efficiency and cost-effectiveness.
