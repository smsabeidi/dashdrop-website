Key Features

Roadie Same Day® includes a comprehensive suite of features designed to make same-day delivery easy and efficient.

Flexibility

On-Demand or Scheduled

Choose the delivery option that best fits your needs. Offer on-demand delivery for urgent orders or schedule deliveries in advance for greater efficiency.

Reach

Deliver Up to 100 Miles

Expand your delivery radius and reach more customers with the extensive Roadie network. Deliver up to 100 miles from your pickup location.

Efficiency

Optimized Delivery Consolidation

Reduce delivery times and costs with optimized consolidation technology. The Roadie platform automatically consolidates deliveries into efficient batches for maximum productivity – at pick-up and drop-off.

Visibility

Real-Time Tracking

Keep track of every delivery in real-time. Get instant updates on delivery status and estimated arrival times, plus get additional visibility tools as you scale across the U.S.

Customizable

Your Brand, Your Delivery

Maintain control of your brand and white-label the customer experience. Customize email and SMS notifications, and offer real-time tracking with your branding.

Integrate

Seamlessly Connect to Systems

Integrate Roadie Same Day® with your existing systems for a seamless delivery experience. The Roadie platform offers easy integration with various e-commerce, order management platforms, TMS providers and more via a public API.

Scale Your Business

Meet Growing Demand for Same-Day

Roadie Same Day® makes it easy to scale your delivery operations to meet growing demand for faster delivery. With a flexible platform and nationwide network, you can deliver tens of orders or thousands of orders every day easily and efficiently.

Reduce Costs

Optimize Your Same-Day Logistics

Streamline your last-mile delivery and reduce costs with Roadie Same Day®. The platform helps you optimize pick-ups, consolidate same-day orders, minimize handling fees and improve overall efficiency.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Roadie Same Day® work?

Roadie Same Day™ works by connecting businesses with a network of independent drivers to fulfill on-demand or scheduled deliveries. You can post your deliveries to the Roadie system through API or bulk upload, and the algorithm will match deliveries with the best drivers, optimizing consolidations for timely delivery.

What are the benefits of using Roadie Same Day®?

Using Roadie Same Day® offers several benefits for businesses, including increased sales, as 82% of consumers are more likely to purchase from retailers offering same-day delivery. Roadie gives you access to a flexible delivery network, real-time tracking, and an efficient system that allows businesses to meet customer demand for same-day delivery more efficiently.

How much does Roadie Same Day® cost?

Enjoy peace of mind with flat-rate pricing, based on your delivery’s size, distance, urgency and pick-up location. Log in to the Roadie platform to get an instant quote. 

How do I get started with Roadie Same Day®?

Businesses can create a free account and start delivering immediately. Visiting our Getting Started page where you'll find all the details to set up your first delivery.

What types of items can be delivered with Roadie Same Day®?

You can deliver a wide range of items, including repair parts, new tires, small furniture, prescriptions, perishables and more. Roadie is a great solution for businesses across industries, including  retail, grocery, healthcare and auto parts.

What areas does Roadie Same Day® cover?

Roadie Same Day® covers a vast area, reaching 97% of U.S. households across more than 30,000 zip codes. This extensive coverage includes both urban and rural areas, ensuring your deliveries can reach a wide audience.

Deliver Bigger, Deliver Better

With Roadie Same Day®, you can deliver more items, more reliably  than with any other solution. The nationwide Roadie network and flexible platform let you deliver big, bulky items, up to 100 miles, with customizable delivery options that fit your customers and your logistics.
