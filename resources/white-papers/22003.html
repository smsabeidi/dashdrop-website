Big & Bulky Done Better: Top challenges and how businesses are solving them

Download the Report Now

When it comes to home delivery, big and bulky merchandise has always been a bit of a misfit.

Their non-standard dimensions often result in slower movement and higher costs through distribution facilities and delivery networks. These obstacles hinder retailers’ ability to convert buyers, offer convenient delivery, and efficiently manage the complex distribution process involving multiple transportation partners.

The burden is so significant that over one-third (36.7%) of the 150 retailers, e-tailers and other businesses surveyed by Roadie and Supply Chain Dive’s studioID in this new report have even restricted delivery for certain oversized items.
