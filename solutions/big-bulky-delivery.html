Frequently Asked Questions

What is considered "big and bulky"?

Roadie enables businesses to deliver items up to 12 feet long and 300 lbs and consumers up to 150 lbs. This includes things like armchairs, minifridges, exercise bikes, ladders, new tires or almost any other large or awkward items.

How does Roadie enable big and bulky deliveries?

Roadie gives you access to a nationwide network of independent drivers with the right vehicles and equipment to handle big and bulky deliveries efficiently. Drivers are certified for large item deliveries, allowing you to deliver items in good condition, on-time. 

Why should I use Roadie for big and bulky delivery?

There are several reasons to choose Roadie for your big and bulky delivery needs:

  • Cost-effectiveness: Roadie has competitive pricing and transparent rates, without the hidden fees or surcharges associated with traditional carriers.
  • Faster delivery: The Roadie nationwide network and efficient operations enable faster delivery times compared to traditional methods.
  • Simplicity: Roadie can integrate with your existing logistics operations, whether you deliver from stores or DCs. That makes it easy for you to manage your deliveries successfully.
  • Reliability: Roadie has a proven track record of helping businesses improve their large-item delivery programs through reduced damages and greater on-time performance.
  • Customer satisfaction: With faster delivery and fewer damages, you’re ensuring a smooth and hassle-free delivery experience for your customers. 

How much does it cost to deliver big and bulky items with Roadie?

Pricing for big and bulky deliveries is based on several factors, including:

  • Delivery location
  • Delivery distance
  • Item size and weight

You can get an instant quote for your specific delivery by creating a Roadie account. Businesses with more than 25 weekly deliveries can contact Roadie sales for volume pricing.

Can I track my big and bulky deliveries with Roadie?

Yes, you can track your big and bulky deliveries with Roadie real-time tracking. You'll receive updates on your delivery’s progress, including estimated delivery times and real-time progress

Which Roadie product is right for my big and bulky deliveries?

Roadie has two main solutions for big and bulky deliveries:

  • Roadie Same Day®: Ideal for on-demand delivery within 4-6 hours or for ship-from-store deliveries of big and bulky items.
  • RoadieXD™: Designed for high-volume same-day delivery from fulfillment centers of big and bulky items, leveraging a network of cross-docks for greater efficiency. RoadieXD™ requires a minimum of 50 deliveries per pickup.
The Roadie sales team can help you assess your needs and recommend the most suitable solution.
