Frequently Asked Questions

What types of construction supplies can I deliver with Roadie?

Roadie enables delivery of a wide variety of construction supplies, from small tools and fasteners to large and bulky materials. Some examples include, lumber, plywood, piping, fittings, drywall, insulation, tools, paint, roofing materials, landscaping supplies and more. Deliver items up to 300 lbs and 12 feet in length.

Can Roadie handle oversized or heavy materials safely?

Yes, several features make delivering oversized or heavy items fast and easy with Roadie:

  • Variety of Vehicles: The network includes drivers with a variety of vehicle types, from pickup trucks to cargo vans and box trucks, to accommodate different sizes and weights.
  • Driver Experience: Many drivers have experience handling construction materials and are equipped to load and unload them safely.
  • Equipment: Drivers can be certified to have equipment such as hand trucks, dollies, and straps to secure and move materials.
Drivers do not have lift gates and cannot load or unload palletized material.

What is the cost of using Roadie for construction supplies delivery?

Pricing for each delivery is based on the delivery size, location, distance and urgency. Create a free business account to get an instant quote. For high volume pricing, contact the Roadie sales team.

What are the benefits of using Roadie for construction supplies delivery?

Roadie creates numerous benefits for construction supplies delivery:

  • Increased Efficiency: Get materials to the job site faster, reducing downtime and keeping projects on schedule.
  • Flexibility: Deliver a wide range of materials, from small parts to oversized items.
  • Cost Savings: Potentially save on delivery costs compared to traditional carriers or maintaining your own fleet.
  • Reliability: The network of drivers provides reliable and timely delivery service.

How can I get started with Roadie for my construction supplies deliveries?

Getting started with Roadie is easy. Create a free business account and send your first delivery within minutes. If you have more than 25 weekly deliveries, contact the Roadie sales team to learn about even more features to optimize your last-mile logistics.
